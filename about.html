About

My Story

Hello! I'm Lexi, a UI Designer from Baltimore Maryland. Growing up in a military family I was lucky enough to travel a lot as a kid, and grow my world view. When I'm not working, I'm hanging out with my dog Winnebago, going to farmers markets and practicing the many crafts I'm always working on. I've had a passion for design my whole life, but have only recently given myself the space to learn and grow.

While working in tech for the last few years I came to learn about UI and UX design. It was a world I didn't know existed, but I knew I wanted to be a part of it. I've spend the majority of my career in customer facing roles, such as retail and operations. Moving into a path that focuses on the users needs just made too much sense to ignore.
