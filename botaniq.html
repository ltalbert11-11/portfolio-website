Botaniq

Botaniq is a an e-commerce app for plant shoppers. With a user friendly design and the options to filter fo exactly what the user is looking for, Botaniq is just what the horticulturist ordered.

With plants becoming the new pets, more and more beginners are looking for an easy place to start. Not being able to sort by amount of care needed, pet safety, and light needed makes it harder for users to get into the hobby.

Creating user flows helps narrow down exactly what the user needs to do to complete their task.

Low and Mid fidelity Wireframes are important in the prototyping of projects. Creating a simple visual to catch any issues, before putting too much time into a faulty feature

High fidelity wireframes give the client, and potential users an opportunity to see exactly what the project will look and how it will function.
